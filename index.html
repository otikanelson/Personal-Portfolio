About me

I'm Otika Nelson Somto, a passionate Software Engineering student at Babcock University with a strong foundation in mobile and web development. My journey in tech has been marked by hands-on experience, from interning at NIIT to freelancing and working on diverse projects. I've developed Android applications, created interactive websites, and even built a JavaFX-based paint application. My skills span Java, C/C++, HTML, CSS, SQL, JavaScript, and Kotlin, with proficiency in tools like Android Studio, Git, and IntelliJ. Beyond coding, I'm an active member of my university's Coding Club, Java Republic Club, and Google Developers Student Club, where I continue to expand my knowledge and collaborate with fellow tech enthusiasts. I'm driven by a passion for creating user-friendly, innovative solutions and am always eager to take on new challenges in the ever-evolving world of software development.
